Kaala Khatta Recipe

Description:

The best drink to make and drink espeically during summer times. Taste a mix of bitter yet sweet and refreshing drink made from the syrups of Jamun.

Ingredients:

  • Kaala khatta syrup bottle
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Hajmola packet
  • 1/2 Lemon
  • Cold water/sparkling water
  • Spoon

Steps:

  1. Fill the glass 1/4th with the kaala khatta syrup
  2. Fill the brim of the glass with either cold or sparkling water
  3. Add salt, pepper and hajmola packet to it
  4. Mix it well with a spoon, let it sizzle
  5. Add mixed drops of the half lemon
  6. Stir it well and immediatly drink it/chug it.
