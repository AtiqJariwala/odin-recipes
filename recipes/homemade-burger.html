Homemade Burger Recipe

Description:

One of the best home made burgers you will ever taste. Just follow the recipe, grab a can of coke and enjoy it! (Do not forget the can of coke since the burger feels incomplete without it!)

Ingredients:

  • 2 Burger Buns
  • Mayonnaise
  • Ketchup
  • Honey Mustard
  • Lettuce
  • Pickles
  • Meat patty

Steps:

  1. Spread mayo and ketchup on both sides of the buns
  2. Place the lettuce on one of the buns
  3. Place the meat patty on top of the lettuce
  4. Place pickles and onions on top of the meat patty
  5. Close it with the other bun
  6. Mix mayo, ketchup and honey mustard in a bowl to create a sauce to dip and eat with the burger
